Welcome to AlgMatch
This app was developed as a part of final year projects at the University of Glasgow.

The project

This app is a matching algorithm animator proposed by Sofiat Olaosebikan, the supervisor for this project, and developed and initally developed by Liam Lau as part of his final year individual project which took place over the course of 6 months. The idea of this app was to create a slick, easy to use learning tool for matching algorithms - that is, algorithms that involves the stable matching of groups through various criteria.

The app was then further extended as part of my final yeat project, again under the supervision of Sofiat Olaosebikan. Over the course of 6 months varouis features were added, modified and updated. Although this project is not as polished or feature-rich as I would like it to be, due to time and other constraints, I hope you like it and are also able to give me some feedback on what I could improve!

This app is created for desktop use - so will look the best on larger screens - there are currently no plans to optimise it for mobile devices so elements may not appear properly on smaller screens/windows.

To get started or learn more, head over to the Algorithms page where you can select an algorithm to run in different scenarios. If the algorithm you are looking for it not there this may be added in future updates.
